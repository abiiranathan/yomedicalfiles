About us

Yo Medical Files(U) Ltd is a private software company limited by shares registered in Uganda. We were registered officially by UNBS on 31st July 2020.

Our core vision is to be Ugandan's leading software company dedicated to designing agile, affordable, and robust software for individuals, teams and organizations. We specialize in designing medical software like Health Management Systems using modern technologies at a very affordable fee compared to market standards. This has enabled small-medium sized clinics to harness the power automated patient management without operating outside their small budgets. Our company is driven by a small team of Developers and Medical doctors whom we collaborate with to provide professional medical care to patients using some of our services.

What we do

  • Designing desktop, web and mobile applications.
  • Providing technical support to software teams specializing in Python, JavaScript, NodeJS, Java, Android and all web technologies.
  • Throw our Eclinic Health Management System, we provide support to multiple clinics with a robust HMIS to cater for their patient and instituitional needs.
  • To lay our brick in the fight for cardiovascular disease morbidity and mortality, we designed an android application to help people with hypertension and diabetes monitor their blood pressure and glucose and to offer professional advice through phone calls and Whatsapp messaging.
